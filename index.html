What's the process?

Enhance efficiency in design by breaking projects into smaller tasks.

Consultation

In-depth discussion between the client and the Web Developer. Providing insights and guidance regarding technical feasibility, design trends, and potential solutions.

Prototype

A mockup of the website is created based on the discussed requirements. The client reviews the prototype and provides feedback on aspects such as layout, functionality, and aesthetics.

Development

The developer proceeds with the actual development of the site until both parties are satisfied with the final product. The client is kept updated on the progress and may request revisions or adjustments as needed.
